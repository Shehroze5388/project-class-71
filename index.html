campus life

Spacious and A/C auditorium

It has a capacity of upto 100 people at a time and was opened in 2017(building changed)

The audotorium has 3 A/C's, each of 2 tons. There is a stage-like raised area at the end of the auditorium so that the awaredees, guests and anchors can stand at the stage as per the event or occasion. I have been on the stage many times mostly for getting awards or badges.






Very big and A/C library

It can fit upto 50 people at the very large oval-shaped table in the center of the library

The library is at the 1st floor of the Secondary school building. There is a librarian who is always present in the labrary till the school hours. The are huge and large shelves dedicated for books and encyclopedias of respective subjects and topics. Both the students of the Primary and Secondary sections share the same library.I like to go to our school library because there I can read books as per my choice and also do my homework/assignment if I have to submit any of them on the same day. I also like the librarian because he is very good and friendly.

Large playground and playing area

The playground is very big and there is also a dedicated sports room.

The playground is just beside the river "Ganga'. There are huge tall walls at the boundaries so that no one can go to the river-side. It lies at left of the Senior school building.There are two goal posts in the playground for playing football and both indoor and outdoor sports accescories and equipments are present in the game room.




Achievements of our school in the last 2 years

The girls of our school came 1st in the Inter District Parade for Girls. It was a pround moment for our school because the girls of our schooll defended their title for the 3rd time in a row and had won 5times before that streak of titles.

First in the Inter District Football Competition(Juniors)

After the seniors of our school won the competition, the junior team also showed their class and they too won it for the school.
